Community Icons

For this project the goal was to make recognizable icons for local community services such as, community fridges, food pantries, community gardens, and food stamps. The final product we then be displayed on a map, using these icons to show people within the community where these resources are located in the area. I used figma to create the icons, playing around with basic, recognizable shapes to demonstrate the community service.
