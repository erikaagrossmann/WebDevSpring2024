Multimedia Hand Project

This piece was part of a 12 week constant output project. Every week we would create something slightly different within the same theme. For this week, I collabed with two other people and made a multimedia project. We jused the laser cutter to cut the squares perfectly even and then glued them together. The heart was made using colored pencil, marker, and crayon. Finally it was all put together and wrapped with red string.
