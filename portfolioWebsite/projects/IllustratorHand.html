Illustrator Hand

This piece was part of a 12 week constant output project. Every week we would create something slightly different within the same theme. For this week, I had used illustrator to recreate the hand. I used the pen tool to draw the shapes of the hand, shadows, nails, and veins and then took the eyedropper to get the color that I felt fit best in that space.
