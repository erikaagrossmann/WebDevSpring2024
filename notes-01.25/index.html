Buttermilk Pancakes!

This is a description of the recipe:)

Ingredient list

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 stick butter
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 egg
  • Spices
    • Cinnamon
    • Salt
    • Nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Mix well
  2. Heat pan on medium, melt 1 tbsp butter
  3. Add a scoop of batter to the pan
  4. Cook until golden brown
  5. More steps to make the perfect pancake
    1. Count to 10
    2. Flip the pancake
    3. Count to 10
    4. Take out of pan
